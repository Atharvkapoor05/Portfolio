About

I'm an aspiring Data Analyst based in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, with hands-on experience turning raw, messy datasets into decisions people can act on. My toolkit centers on SQL and PostgreSQL for heavy-lifting analysis, Python for data wrangling, and Power BI for telling the story once the numbers are clean.

I've built end-to-end analytics projects across 1M+ records, applying CTEs, window functions, cohort analysis, churn analysis, and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) modelling to solve real business reporting problems — not just toy datasets.

A Minor in Management alongside my B.Tech means I don't just write queries; I think about the business question behind them — stakeholder needs, KPIs, and what a dashboard actually needs to say to be useful.
